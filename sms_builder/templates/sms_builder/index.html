What We Cover

Compliance Coverage

Our system helps businesses manage the key compliance areas required for heavy vehicle safety, operational control, and documented risk management.

01

HVNL Alignment

Support for Heavy Vehicle National Law obligations, policies, and documented procedures.

02

Chain of Responsibility

Capture responsibilities across schedulers, operators, drivers, and subcontractors.

03

Risk Assessments

Identify hazards, assess risks, and maintain mitigation records in one system.

04

Driver & Staff Records

Maintain licence details, inductions, training, and competency records for all staff.

05

Maintenance & Inspections

Track vehicle inspections, maintenance activities, and safety check documentation.

06

Audit Readiness

Prepare evidence, reports, and document sets to support internal and external audits.
